1. Interpolation

Suppose that \(I(x,y)\) is the intensity value of a pixel at the discrete position \((x, y)\) of image \(I\). Interpolation is the process of estimating the intensity value at continuous coordinates \((u, v)\), written as \(I(u, v)\).

Interpolation can be applied in many applications such as scaling, rotation, and warping.
