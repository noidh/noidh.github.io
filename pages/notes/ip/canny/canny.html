1. Introduction

The process of Canny edge detection algorithm consists of 5 different steps:

  1. Apply Gaussian filter to denoise.
  2. Find the intensity gradients of the image.
  3. Compute gradient's maginute and theta, then apply a non-maximal suppression to remove undesired edges.
  4. Apply double threshold to determine potential edges.
  5. Perform edge tracking by hysteresis to detect real edge.
